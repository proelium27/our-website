# Product Marketing Context
 
*Last updated: 2026-03-23*
 
## Brand Name

**Why:** Small business owners know something is wrong with their online presence but feel stuck. Fixable signals that the problem is solvable — and that we're the ones to solve it.
**Tagline direction:** "Your online presence, fixed." or "We fix websites. You fix everything else."

**What it does:** Fixable is a web agency that creates, refines, and maintains websites for small businesses. We handle everything from design to launch, delivering polished results that DIY tools and solo freelancers can't match.
**Product category:** Web design & development agency
**Product type:** Agency / service
**Location:** Bethesda, MD (serving the DMV — DC, Maryland, Virginia)
**Primary market:** Local small businesses in the DMV metro area
**Geographic expansion:** Remote-friendly — can serve clients nationally but local DMV businesses are the primary target for initial traction
**Business model:**
- **Build & Hand Off** — one-time fee, we build it, you own it

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Small businesses — contractors, HVAC, retail, restaurants, and similar local/trade businesses
**Decision-makers:** The business owner
**Geographic focus:** DMV metro area (DC, Maryland, Virginia) — dense concentration of small businesses, high median income, competitive local search environment means clients feel the pain of weak online presence acutely
**Primary use case:** Getting a professional website to attract online customers
**Jobs to be done:**
- Establish a credible online presence
- Tap into the consumer base that finds businesses online
- Look as professional as larger competitors
 
## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Small business owners see competitors winning online and want in, but can't produce anything professional on their own.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- DIY tools (Wix, Squarespace, AI builders) produce generic, low-quality results that don't reflect the business well
- Freelancers are unreliable — one person who might disappear
**What it costs them:** Lost customers who can't find them online; falling behind competitors
**Emotional tension:** Embarrassed by what they'd produce themselves; frustrated that professional help seems out of reach
 
## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Local freelancers — fall short because they're a single point of failure with no team behind them
**Secondary:** DIY tools (Wix, Squarespace, AI builders) — fall short because they produce template-looking, low-quality sites
**Indirect:** Doing nothing / staying offline — fall short because they're losing customers to competitors every day
 
**Named competitors (researched):**
- **Pronto Marketing** — WordPress-based, generic "small business" positioning with no vertical depth
- **OneNine** — WordPress, maintenance-focused but no performance differentiation
- **Hibu** — Large, opaque pricing, WordPress stack, commodity feel
- **Thrive / Straight North / WebFX** — All hide pricing behind sales calls; WordPress-centric
- **HVAC Web Works** — Ultra-niche (contractors only), can't serve adjacent verticals
 
**The shared weakness across all:** Nearly every competitor runs on WordPress — creating real, documentable problems: plugin vulnerabilities, slow load times, update failures, downtime. None lead with performance as a business outcome.
 
## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Two co-founders who are directly hands-on with every project — no account managers, no handoffs, no miscommunication
- Professional quality — no templates or AI slop, actual skilled development
- Two service tiers that fit different owner needs
- **Vercel-deployed sites** — faster by default, no CMS attack surface, no plugin vulnerabilities (technical moat competitors can't easily replicate without rebuilding their delivery model)
- **Transparent, published pricing** — most mid-to-large competitors hide pricing behind a sales call; publishing real numbers is a conversion differentiator
- **Performance as a business outcome** — "faster site = higher Google Maps ranking = more phone calls" — nobody in the space owns this narrative
**How we do it differently:** We treat every small business like a real client with real goals, not a template to fill in. We deploy on Vercel instead of WordPress, so sites are faster, safer, and cheaper to maintain by default.
**Why that's better:** Owners get something they're proud of, built by a reliable team that won't disappear — and it loads in under a second instead of 4+
**Why customers choose us:** Professional results at accessible prices, transparent pricing, and a technical stack that measurably outperforms WordPress alternatives on Core Web Vitals
 
**Core positioning statement:** Fixable builds fast, modern websites for local businesses — and keeps them running. Unlike WordPress agencies, our sites launch on Vercel, load in under a second, and don't get hacked.
 
**Performance proof point (use this):** "Your competitor's WordPress site loads in 4.2 seconds. Ours loads in 0.8 seconds. Google ranks the faster site." — demonstrable, not just a marketing claim.
