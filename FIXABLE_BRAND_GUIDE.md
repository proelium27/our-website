## Who We Are

**Fixable** is a web design agency founded by two high school co-founders — Caleb and Jack Rebello — based in Bethesda, MD. We build fast, modern websites for small local businesses in the DMV (DC, Maryland, Virginia) region.

We are NOT a large agency. We are two developers who move fast, care about quality, and deliver sites that actually perform — unlike the WordPress shops that dominate this market.

## Our Mission

Get local DMV businesses off slow, outdated WordPress sites and onto modern, high-performance websites that rank on Google, load in under a second, and turn visitors into customers.

## Outreach Voice & Tone

### Script Selection Rules

| Business Type | Script to Use | Tone |
|---|---|---|
| Law offices, dental, med spas, corporate | **Script 1 — Professional** | Confident, direct, credible |
| Restaurants, salons, barbershops, contractors | **Script 2 — Narrative** | Warm, personal, disarming |

### Script 1 — Professional (Cold Email)
**Subject:** free website for [business name]

> Hey [name],
>
> I came across [business name] online and wanted to reach out — I put together a free website mockup for you, no strings attached.
>
> It's built out with your branding and designed to help you show up better on Google and turn visitors into paying clients.
>
> If you want to take a look, just reply and I'll send it over.
>
> — Caleb

**Day 3 follow-up:**
> Hey [name], just wanted to bump this up in case it got buried. The mockup is ready whenever you want to see it — takes 30 seconds to look at. Worth it?

**Day 7 follow-up:**
> Last follow up from me — I put real time into building this for you so I figured I'd give it one more shot. If the timing's just not right, totally understand. Either way the offer stands whenever you're ready.

---

### Script 2 — Narrative (Cold Email)
**Subject:** we built something for you

> Hey [name],
>
> This is probably not what you're used to getting in your inbox — my name is Caleb and I'm a sophomore in high school. Me and my friend spent the last year teaching ourselves how to build AI-powered websites for local businesses.
>
> We came across [business name] and honestly it stood out to us — so we went ahead and built you a free website mockup just to show you what we could do. No charge, no strings attached.
>
> We're two kids trying to build something real and the best way we know how to do that is just show people what we can make before we ask for anything.
>
> Want me to send it over?
>
> — Caleb

**Day 3 follow-up:**
> Hey [name] — just following up. We put real work into this mockup and genuinely think you'd like it. Takes 30 seconds to look at. Want me to send it over?

**Day 7 follow-up:**
> Last one from me — we're two high school kids trying to build something from scratch and this is how we're doing it. If the timing's not right I completely get it. But if you ever want to see what we built for you, the offer stands.

## Non-Negotiable Rules for All Agents

1. **Never mention pricing unprompted** — the mockup offer comes first, always
2. **Never send anything without human approval** — Caleb reviews all outreach before it goes out
3. **Always personalize** — reference the specific business, their weakness, their vertical
4. **One ask per message** — the only ask is: "want me to send it over?" or "what's the best email?"
5. **Never claim we've already built their mockup unless we actually have**
6. **Tone must match the script** — don't mix professional and narrative in the same email
7. **Geographic focus is DMV** — do not prospect businesses outside DC, Maryland, or Virginia
